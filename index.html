Welcome to Personal Pages of Robin Goerlach on GitHub.

As you can easily guess, and have probably already guessed, I'm Robin Görlach. I work as a software developer and Linux administrator at SASD-GmbH, Darmstadt (Germany).

Software Development

As a software developer, I work with C#, Java, PHP, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript in web applications. The SASD uses Oracle, MySQL, and PostgreSQL as supported database development platforms, but we have also developed for some more exotic systems like Firebird.

Unix Admin

Currently, I'm working with CentOS, Debian, Ubuntu, and openSUSE as a Linux admin. In the old days of Unix history, I worked with Irix and Solaris, but that is long-forgotten knowledge. What remains of Unix are FreeBSD and macOS.

I even had the pleasure of working with OpenVMS, but that is not Unix and is a whole different story.
